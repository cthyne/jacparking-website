About Us

Locally owned and family operated

Our company JAC Management was founded in 2022, when we realized a goal to start a company that would afford our children and future generations a healthy quality of life here in Hawaii. Using lessons learned from the past to guide our future, we do believe that there is a right way to provide quality service. We focus on the needs of our customers as the standard of our business. Utilizing honesty, integrity and family values as our foundation, we look to provide professional management services to our community.

We believe that hard work and having good work ethic will lead to success. Our comprehensive management experience and lean management approach makes us the perfect company for all your management needs. Let's make sure that it is done right the first time.
